Services

We provide following services to our personal and professional customers.

Computer Repairs & Upgrades

Is your computer freezing, crashing, or refusing to turn on? We troubleshoot and repair all types of hardware and software issues for desktops and laptops, including both Windows and Mac systems. Whether it’s a failing hard drive, faulty RAM, or a damaged motherboard, we’ll get your system running like new. We also offer upgrades to improve speed, performance, and functionality—so you don’t have to buy a whole new device.

Hardware & Software Upgrades

Technology evolves fast—and so can your computer. We offer expert advice and installation of upgrades like SSDs, RAM, processors, and operating systems to extend the life of your device. Whether you need more storage, faster boot times, or the latest software, we’ve got you covered. We only recommend upgrades that offer real performance benefits and value.

Data Recovery & Backup Solutions

Lost important files or can’t access your hard drive? We use advanced tools and techniques to recover lost documents, photos, and data from damaged or failing storage devices. In addition to recovery, we provide secure and automated backup solutions to protect your files from future data loss. Whether it’s a personal laptop or a business server, your data is safe with us.

Virus & Malware Removal

If your device is running slow, displaying strange pop-ups, or acting suspiciously, it could be infected with malware. We thoroughly scan and remove all types of viruses, spyware, and ransomware without risking your files. After removal, we’ll secure your system and recommend protective software to keep threats out. Your digital safety is our top priority.

Small Business Office Setup

Starting a new business or upgrading your office? We specialize in setting up efficient and secure office environments tailored to small businesses. From configuring workstations and network setups to installing essential software and hardware, we ensure everything runs smoothly from day one. We also provide consultation on the best tech solutions for your business needs, helping you make the most out of your budget.

Managed IT Services

Running a business? We provide ongoing, proactive IT support for small businesses across Edmonton, including remote monitoring, software updates, system security, and network management. Our managed IT plans ensure your tech works smoothly—minimizing downtime and maximizing productivity. Think of us as your outsourced IT department—reliable, responsive, and cost-effective.
